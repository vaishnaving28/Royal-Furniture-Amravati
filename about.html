Our Story

A journey of passion, craftsmanship, and dedication to creating timeless furniture.

The Royal Beginning

Royal Furniture began as a small workshop in 1998, founded by Mr. Rajesh Sharma, a passionate craftsman with a vision to create furniture that combined traditional Indian craftsmanship with contemporary design sensibilities.

Starting with just three artisans in a modest workshop in Amravati, Maharashtra, the focus was always on quality over quantity. Each piece was meticulously crafted by hand, paying close attention to detail and finish.

Over the years, as word spread about the exceptional quality and design of our furniture, demand grew, and so did our team. Today, Royal Furniture stands as Amravati's premier furniture destination with a team of over 50 skilled craftsmen and designers.
